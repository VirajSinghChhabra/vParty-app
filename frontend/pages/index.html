Watch Netflix Together, through vParty

Synchronize your Netflix viewing experience with friends and family, no matter where they are.

Get Started

Features

Synchronized Playback

Watch in perfect sync with your friends, pause and resume together.

Built-in Chat

Chat with your watch party without leaving the Netflix interface.

Secure Connection

Your watch parties are private and secure using peer-to-peer technology.

How It Works

1. Install the Extension

Add our Chrome extension to your browser.

2. Create or Join a Party

Start a new watch party or join an existing one with a simple click.

3. Enjoy Together

Watch, chat, and enjoy Netflix content in perfect sync with your friends.
